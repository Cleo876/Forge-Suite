A New Philosophy for Software

Tired of losing access to your tools and data when subscriptions end? The Forge Suite is our answer. Inspired by a call to create lasting value, our Community Edition tools are distributed as single, self-contained HTML files.

Truly Offline-First

All tools run entirely in your browser. No internet connection is required after the initial file load. Your work is your own, stored on your machine.

Genuinely Open Source

Download the HTML file, you own the code. Learn from it, remix it, and even sell your own versions. The tool is yours to command.

Empowerment Over Lock-in

We challenge the model that revokes your access if you stop paying. Our Community Edition tools are free forever. You always keep what you have.

The Creator & The Charge

A "pragmatic idealist" building tools that empower, not entrap.

About the Creator

The Forge Suite is created and maintained by Cleon Williams, a developer and engineer with over a decade of experience. His expertise spans from his first skill, web development, to game development and automation systems engineering (n8n).

This diverse technical background is united by a core philosophy of "pragmatic idealism" and a passion for technological philanthropy, with the Forge Suite being his most ambitious philanthropic work to date.

The tools you see are a genuine feat of engineering. Creating complex, stateful applications like ERD Forge and FrameForge as single, self-contained HTML files is a difficult, "unorthodox" choice, reflecting a deep mastery of browser-native technologies over convenient crutches. This approach isn't a gimmick; it's the core of the mission.

The Forge Philosophy

The "why" behind Forge is stronger than most. This mission, described by Cleon as a "charge from God," is to build tools that empower rather than entrap.

It is an active counter-narrative to the dominant "rent-seeking" SaaS model, which often leads to digital dispossessionâ€”where your access to your *own work* can be revoked by a price hike or a policy change.

The Forge Suite is built on two simple, powerful principles:

  • The Public Good: The Community Edition tools are a gift. They are free forever, and by their very architecture, they *cannot* be taken back.
  • The Sustainable Engine: The Partner Program is the engine that funds the dream. Revenue from businesses who need custom, server-side solutions directly pays for the R&D to create the next generation of open-source tools for everyone.

Why "Forge"?

"Forge" signifies our core identity: crafting robust, powerful, and enduring software, much like a blacksmith methodically shapes raw material into a permanent, high-value tool.

This name also extends that power to you, the user. We provide you with your own "forge" to shape and build your ideas with a guarantee of lasting ownership, free from external control.
